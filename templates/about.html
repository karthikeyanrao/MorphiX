Campus Live Dashboard

Your real-time window into campus resources and updates

What is it?

Campus Live Dashboard helps students and faculty share timely information about canteens, libraries, labs, events and other facilities. See crowd levels, queue times and important notices at a glance so you can plan your day better.

Live Updates

Quickly check crowd levels, queue length and importance for places across campus.

Community Driven

Students and faculty contribute updates and vote to surface the most helpful posts.

Photo Uploads

Add images to posts for better context. Images are stored securely in Supabase Storage.

Secure & Simple

Authentication is powered by Supabase Auth; only owners can edit their posts.

How it works

  1. Login or register
  2. Create a post with title, description and optional photo
  3. Add live status: Crowd, Queue and Importance
  4. Vote and comment to help others

Backend

Flask app with Supabase (Postgres, Auth, Storage) for data and files.

Frontend

Jinja templates with a clean, responsive design and rich UI components.

Purpose

Save time, avoid crowds, and keep the campus community informed in real time.
